# Jay Durga Trade International — Website Guide

Welcome! This folder contains the official website for **Jay Durga International Trade**, Kathmandu’s trusted importer and distributor of premium skincare, cosmetics, and personal care brands.

This guide is written for **business owners, staff, and partners** — no technical background needed.

---

## What This Website Is For

The website helps visitors:

- Learn about Jay Durga International Trade and our story  
- Browse **19+ global brands** we import and distribute in Nepal  
- Explore product categories (Skincare, Cosmetics, Personal Care)  
- See why retailers and partners trust us  
- **Contact us** easily by phone, WhatsApp, or email  

---

## What Visitors Can Do on the Site

| Section | What it shows |
|--------|----------------|
| **Home** | Introduction, featured product areas, and quick links |
| **About Us** | Our mission, vision, and operating values |
| **Brands Showcase** | All brands we represent — searchable and filterable |
| **Categories** | Skincare, cosmetics, and personal care groupings |
| **Why Choose Us** | Authenticity, partnerships, portfolio, and quality |
| **Contact** | Office details, phone, WhatsApp, and email |

On mobile, brand and category sections appear in an easy **two-column** layout. A green **WhatsApp** button is always visible at the bottom-right for quick messages.

## Viewing the Website on Your Computer

If someone on your team has set up the site for local preview:

1. Open the project folder on the computer  
2. Follow the instructions from your developer to start the site  
3. Open a web browser and go to: **http://localhost:3000**

> **Note:** `localhost` means the site is running on that computer only — it is not yet live on the internet until it is published to a web host.

---

## Publishing the Website Online (Live for Everyone)

To put the website on the internet (e.g. `www.yourdomain.com`), you will need:

1. A **domain name** (website address)  
2. A **hosting service** (where the site files are stored)  
3. A **web developer or agency** to upload and maintain the site  

Share this project folder with your developer. They can build and deploy the site to your chosen hosting provider.

---

## Updating Content (Brands, Text, Contact Info)

Most website text and brand listings are managed in the project files. **We recommend asking a developer to make changes** so nothing breaks.

Common updates you may request:

- Add or remove a brand  
- Change phone number or email  
- Update office address or business hours  
- Replace photos or product category images  

Keep a list of changes and send them to your developer in writing (email or WhatsApp).
